The **AI Interview Proctoring and Mock Interview Assessment System** is an intelligent web-based application developed using Python, Django, OpenCV, and Artificial Intelligence technologies to improve the process of conducting online interviews. With the increasing use of remote hiring, companies face challenges such as cheating, lack of proper monitoring, and difficulty in evaluating candidates fairly. This system addresses those problems by providing a secure and automated interview environment where candidates can attend interviews remotely while being monitored by AI-based proctoring technology. The system begins by collecting candidate details such as job role, job description, and years of experience. Based on this information, the system automatically generates relevant interview questions using an AI language model, making the interview process more dynamic and tailored to the specific job role.

During the interview session, the candidate’s webcam is activated and the system continuously monitors the candidate using computer vision techniques implemented through OpenCV. The AI proctoring module analyzes the video feed in real time and detects suspicious behaviors such as the presence of multiple faces, the candidate leaving the screen, or repeatedly looking away from the screen. These behaviors may indicate cheating or external assistance during the interview. Whenever such activities are detected, the system records them and stores the information in the interview log. This allows recruiters to review the candidate’s behavior after the interview and ensures that the interview process remains fair and transparent.

In addition to monitoring behavior, the system also focuses on evaluating the candidate’s answers. The candidate responds to the generated interview questions, and the system compares the responses with predefined ideal answers stored in the backend. Using Natural Language Processing techniques, the system analyzes the relevance, accuracy, and completeness of the candidate’s answers. Based on this analysis, the system calculates a performance score that helps recruiters understand the candidate’s technical knowledge and communication skills. This automated evaluation reduces the time recruiters spend manually reviewing candidate responses and helps them identify suitable candidates more efficiently.

The system also includes an admin or recruiter dashboard that allows administrators to manage interview sessions and analyze results. Recruiters can view candidate profiles, check interview activity logs, review suspicious behavior alerts, and analyze the final performance scores generated by the system. This centralized dashboard helps recruiters make data-driven hiring decisions and simplifies the recruitment workflow. The platform therefore serves as both an interview monitoring tool and an intelligent candidate assessment system.

Overall, the AI Interview Proctoring System enhances the online recruitment process by combining AI-driven question generation, real-time behavior monitoring, and automated answer evaluation. By integrating technologies such as Django for backend development, OpenCV for computer vision, and AI-based language models for question generation and analysis, the system provides a secure, efficient, and scalable solution for conducting remote interviews. This project demonstrates how modern artificial intelligence technologies can be used to improve the reliability, transparency, and effectiveness of online hiring processes.
